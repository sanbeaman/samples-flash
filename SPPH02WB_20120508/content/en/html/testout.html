Test-Out Options

If you select this option:
  • You may attempt the test-out one time
  • You must complete the test-out in 60 minutes or less
  • You must pass with a score of at least 80 percent
  • If the test-out is passed, the course content is available for review
  • If the test-out is not passed, all course completion requirements apply
 If you select this option:
  • You will proceed directly to the course
  • The test-out will not be offered again
  • You must meet all completion requirements to receive course credit
   
 

Test-Out Confirmation

Although anyone may attempt the Test-Out, you are most likely to be successful if:
  • You have ample time and a distraction-free environment in which to complete it
  • You have gained valuable course-related knowledge through significant job experience
  • You have reviewed and mastered course objectives
Course Objectives:
  • Identify when sales/marketing changes justify staff increases
  • Recognize typical organizational structures
  • Identify equipment and recommended proximity to major stations
  • Identify common storage methods for simple and oversize parts
  • Recognize common options to expand storage and increase its efficiency
