Course Navigation
The course lessons are presented in a linear fashion. You can use the menu to access a specific lesson.

Exit Properly
Be sure to leave the course by selecting the button labeled SAVE/EXIT. Selecting the SAVE/EXIT button will save your current progress and scores. Do not select the X at the top of the browser window until you have properly exited the course. Exiting improperly will result in the loss of current progress and scores.

Progress Tracking
This course keeps track of your progress. As you complete each lesson your progress is indicated as shown below:

Not entered
Entered, but not complete
Complete

You may resume your course progress on a different computer, provided you exit properly.

Scoring
A score of at least 80 percent on all tests is required to receive course credit. You may retake any test as often as you like. Your best score will be retained.
